# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

IA² is for standards-minded web developers, information architects, ontology
authors, and builders of intelligent agents. They arrive looking for a
concrete way to make information inspectable and interoperable without hiding
meaning in a detached payload or surrendering an application to an AI
platform.

## Product Purpose

IA² explores an information architecture for a decentralized agentic web.
Documents and applications should be able to explain what they contain, how
their parts relate, what constraints apply, and where claims came from. The
homepage should make that larger direction clear while leading visitors to
the first working vertical slice: HTML/RDF, its navigator, and a live DOM demo.

## Brand Personality

Legible, rigorous, and quietly optimistic. The voice is technically precise
without sounding institutional, speculative without hype, and ambitious
without pretending that open questions are settled.

## Anti-references

Do not resemble an AI startup sales page, a crypto or cyberpunk interface, a
generic developer-tool template, or an enterprise site filled with abstract
claims. Avoid chatbot imagery, glowing network graphics, feature-card grids,
agentic-web hype, and editorial-magazine affectation.

## Design Principles

1. Practice what IA² proposes: structure and meaning should be visible and
   correlated.
2. Lead with the larger decentralized-web idea, then offer concrete artifacts
   to inspect.
3. Prefer precise claims and useful links over persuasion or marketing gloss.
4. Let open standards feel consequential, current, and human-readable.
5. Keep description, validation, authority, and execution visibly distinct.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA. Preserve full keyboard navigation, strong focus states,
high text contrast, readable line lengths, semantic landmarks, and useful
reduced-motion behavior. Meaning must never depend on color or motion alone.

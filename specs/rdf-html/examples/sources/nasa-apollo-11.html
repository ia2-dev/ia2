Apollo 11

“The Eagle has landed…”

Mission objective

The primary objective of Apollo 11 was to complete a national goal set by President John F. Kennedy on May 25, 1961: perform a crewed lunar landing and return to Earth.

Additional objectives included scientific exploration by the lunar module crew, deployment of experiments, and gathering samples of lunar-surface materials for return to Earth.

Crew

  • Neil Armstrong, Commander
  • Edwin E. “Buzz” Aldrin Jr., Lunar Module Pilot
  • Michael Collins, Command Module Pilot

IA² Navigator browser extension

Support grounded in the source.

Report a problem, inspect the implementation, or follow the current HTML/RDF and HARE proposals.

Report a problem

Use the IA² issue tracker for installation problems, incorrect extraction, inaccessible controls, HARE verification failures, and other extension support.

Include the browser version, the affected page or a minimal reproducible document, what you expected, and what happened. Do not include passwords, authentication tokens, private documents, or other sensitive information in a public issue.

Inspect the extension

IA² Navigator is open source. Its browser adapter, RDF Navigator, and HARE Viewer can be inspected independently.

Privacy questions

Read the IA² Navigator privacy policy for details about local page processing, session storage, remote resource requests, and Chrome Web Store Limited Use compliance. Privacy questions can also be filed in the issue tracker without including private page content.

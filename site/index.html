Information Architecture for Intelligent Agents

Connected information for people, applications, and agents.

Work now moves among people, applications, and agents, through documents, interfaces, workflows, and conversations. IA² connects those surfaces with explicit identities, relationships, evidence, constraints, and possible actions, so each participant gets the form it needs without losing context.

Explore HTML/RDF ↗ Try the live demo

HTML is the first binding. The larger project is information architecture for connected, multi-participant systems.

01 / Hybrid information

Hybrid work needs hybrid information.

A decision may begin in a conversation, change a live interface, cite a document, trigger an application, and be continued by an agent. Treating each surface as isolated text breaks that chain.

IA² keeps presentation, operational state, and semantic structure connected. Each participant can use the form it understands without severing identity, evidence, constraints, or context.

02 / Connected architecture

A shared layer, not a new silo.

Documents, interfaces, APIs, and agent tools contribute different capabilities. These layers connect them without collapsing data, presentation, and execution into one mechanism.

  1. 01

    Surfaces and live state

    Where is information encountered, and what is changing now?

    Documents, interfaces, streams, and host-native bindings.
  2. 02

    Identity and relationships

    What is this, and how does it connect across surfaces?

    RDF 1.2, open vocabularies, profiles, and mappings.
  3. 03

    Integrity and evidence

    What supports it, and what must remain true?

    SHACL, provenance, typed confidence, scoped statements, validation, and signatures.
  4. 04

    Interaction and authority

    What may happen next, under which conditions, and by whose authority?

    Traceable decisions and conversational interaction, with controlled execution.
  5. 05

    Continuity and exchange

    How does context survive across surfaces, sessions, and organizations?

    Discovery, processing lineage, governed artifact evolution, snapshots, diffs, and subscriptions.

04 / Direction

Connected intelligence needs connected information.

Agents will work alongside people and applications, moving among interfaces, documents, tools, and organizations. IA² makes the information connecting that work explicit, portable, inspectable, and open, so no participant has to reconstruct the whole world from prose or surrender it to one platform.
